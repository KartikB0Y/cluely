🔍 Core Characteristics

1. Real-time, context-aware assistance
Cluely listens to the audio and “sees” whatever is on your screen during online interactions, then generates suggested responses, talking points, or answers as the conversation unfolds. It uses AI to interpret context and feed you relevant text you can read or paraphrase.

2. Invisible desktop overlay
Instead of joining a video call or showing a visible UI to other participants, Cluely runs in the background with a transparent overlay, so others typically don’t see that it’s running — even during screen sharing.

3. Live meeting assistance and insight generation
Beyond interview responses, it’s marketed to help with:

taking live meeting notes, summaries, and action items

answering questions in real time

suggesting follow-up points or clarifications

drafting emails after the session

4. Works with common meeting platforms
Cluely integrates with major virtual meeting tools such as Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams by operating at the system level rather than within a specific app
